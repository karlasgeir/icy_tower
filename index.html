Instructions

Controls

  • Use 'A' and 'D' to control the Devil
  • Use SPACE to jump
  • Pause with 'P'

Powerups

  • Powerups:
  • Grab the space suit for super high Moon jumps!
  • Grab the fire to turn into a fireball that goes much faster than the demon!
  • Grab the 'B' to increase the size of the platforms or grab
  • the 'S' if you're up for a challenge, it will make all the
  • platforms on your screen tiny!
  • Watch out for the reaper! He'll try to throw you down the tower!

Score

  • Combos: go more than two floors in one jump to combo!
  • Go as many floors as you can in one combo to increase
  • your combo multiplier and score higher.
  • Grab coins to increase your score.

Clock

  • Don't forget the clock in the upper left corner, when you hear it ring
  • the platforms and your character go a little bit faster! So stay on your
  • toes and hurry up!

Developmental

  • When paused, use 'O' to do steps
  • Press 'T' to get timer
  • Press 'C' to toogle clearing the canvas during rendering
  • Press 'B' to toogle a box for testing purpose
  • Press 'U' to clear a part of the canvas for testing purpose
  • Press 'F' to toogle flip-flip box, that shows a frame counter and if the frame is even or odd
