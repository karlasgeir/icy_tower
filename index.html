Instructions

Controls

  • Use 'A' and 'D' to control the Devil
  • Use SPACE to jump
  • Pause with 'P'
  • Quick restart game by pressing 'R' (Not implemented yet)

Developmental

  • When paused, use 'O' to do steps
  • Press 'T' to get timer
  • Press 'C' to toogle clearing the canvas during rendering
  • Press 'B' to toogle a box for testing purpose
  • Press 'U' to clear a part of the canvas for testing purpose
  • Press 'F' to toogle flip-flip box, that shows a frame counter and if the frame is even or odd
